Welcome to the Stroke Oral Health Initiative.

Our mission is to raise awareness of the important connection between oral health and stroke recovery.

Stroke survivors may experience changes that affect their ability to maintain oral hygiene, increasing the risk of tooth decay, periodontal disease, dry mouth, and difficulties with eating and swallowing.

Through education, advocacy, and collaboration with healthcare professionals, we aim to improve oral health outcomes and quality of life for stroke survivors and their caregivers.

About

The Stroke Oral Health Initiative is dedicated to increasing awareness of the connection between oral health and stroke recovery.

Stroke survivors often experience oral health challenges including xerostomia, dysphagia, reduced dexterity, and increased plaque accumulation.

Our goal is to educate healthcare providers and caregivers on the importance of oral health and provide resources to improve patient outcomes.
